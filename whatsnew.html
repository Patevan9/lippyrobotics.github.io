Actively Growing

What’s New with Scout

Scout is actively growing and improving. This page is here so you can see what has changed, what has improved, and what is being worked on over time.

Scout is not meant to be abandoned after launch. He will continue to receive updates through Google Play as features are improved, issues are fixed, and new companion abilities are carefully added.

Latest Update

Scout is preparing for launch

Scout is currently being finalized for release on Google Play. The focus right now is stability, privacy, family-safe behavior, and making sure Scout works reliably on supported Android devices.


Recent Work

Recent work includes:

  • Improved offline companion behavior
  • Better weather handling for supported U.S. locations
  • Cleaner settings and support screens
  • Improved first-launch welcome information
  • Privacy-first design with no required account
  • Continued testing on real Android hardware
Coming Improvements

What’s ahead

Future Updates May Include
  • Better voice interaction
  • Smoother companion responses
  • Improved memory controls
  • More natural expressions
  • Additional optional customization
  • Better support information inside the app

Scout will grow carefully. Stability comes first.

Update Philosophy

How Scout grows

Scout is designed to be a private, friendly AI companion app. Updates will focus on making Scout more reliable, more helpful, and easier to understand — without turning him into something confusing or unpredictable.

Core companion features will remain the heart of Scout.
