System Requirements

What you need to run Scout.

  • Android 13 or newer
  • 6 GB RAM minimum — 8 GB RAM recommended for the best experience
  • 500 MB storage minimum — 2 GB recommended (more for optional AI brain downloads)
  • Microphone and speaker required
  • Camera optional — required for face and scene recognition features
  • Internet optional — required only for online features such as weather and cloud AI
